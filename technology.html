Four Things about the Technology

Automation
Zunavision’s strength lies in how much it can do automatically. Once it knows where you want the insertion to go, it does the rest. No pre- or post- production, no screens, no graphic design and no video-editing. Anyone can do it.

Movement
Zunavision handles movement beautifully – it identifies and captures the designated space backwards and forwards throughout the video, however much the camera or surface moves and however acute the perspective angles get.

Occlusion
You may have noticed from our demo videos that Zunavision also handles objects and shadows that pass in front of the insertion perfectly – better, in fact, than the green screening technologies used in TV news and weather programs.

Dynamic insertion
Without altering the video itself, the technology can serve the insertion independently from the video while still retaining all the effects mentioned above. So ten people watching the same video at the same time can all be served different ads.
